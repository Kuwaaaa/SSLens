Lumen v2 Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 4, 2026

Lumen v2 ("Lumen", "we", "our") is a browser extension that lets users create contextual Lens cards on webpages and optionally use a lightweight real-time companion mode. This policy explains what information Lumen handles and why.

Information We Collect or Transmit

Lumen may collect, store, or transmit the following information:

  • Your chosen handle, internal user ID, and authentication token.
  • The URL of the webpage where Lumen is used, plus a hashed room ID derived from the canonical URL.
  • Lens content you create, including body text, type, tags, references, reactions, reports, and timestamps.
  • Lens anchoring data, including the selected page text, nearby text context, and text position data needed to restore the Lens on the page.
  • Companion mode presence, emoji events, and temporary chat messages while you actively opt into companion mode.
  • Operational logs that may include IP address, user agent, request path, and timestamps for debugging, abuse prevention, and server security.

How We Use Information

We use this information only to operate Lumen:

  • To authenticate users and keep small-group access working.
  • To fetch, create, display, and synchronize Lens cards for the current webpage.
  • To support reactions, reports, and moderation.
  • To provide opt-in companion mode presence, edge emoji events, and temporary chat.
  • To diagnose bugs, protect the service from abuse, and maintain server reliability.

Anonymous Lens

Posting a Lens as "Anonymous" hides the author from other users in the product interface. It does not make the Lens unlinkable to operators. The server still records the real author so we can handle abuse, reports, and safety issues.

Companion Mode

Companion mode is opt-in. If you do not click "Find companion", you do not appear in companion presence and do not participate in companion chat. Companion emoji and chat events are designed to be ephemeral. Recent chat messages may be kept briefly in server memory so late joiners can see recent context, but companion chat is not intended as durable page history.

Local Browser Storage

Lumen stores your token, handle, reading mode, and visibility preferences in browser extension storage. This lets the extension stay signed in and remember your local settings.

Sharing and Sale of Data

We do not sell personal information. We do not use Lumen data for advertising, ad targeting, or cross-site advertising profiles. We do not share user data with third parties except when necessary to operate hosting infrastructure, comply with law, or protect the service from abuse.

Data Retention

Lens cards are intended to persist on the page where they were created unless deleted through an operator or future product deletion flow. Authentication and operational logs may be retained as needed for service operation, debugging, and abuse prevention. Companion chat is temporary and not stored in the main database by design.

Security

We use reasonable technical measures to protect Lumen data. Before public release, production service traffic should be served over HTTPS and WSS. No internet service can be guaranteed to be perfectly secure.

Your Choices

  • You can choose not to create Lens cards.
  • You can choose not to enter companion mode.
  • You can hide Lumen on a tab or site using extension controls.
  • You can remove the extension to stop Lumen from running in your browser.

Contact

For privacy questions or deletion requests, open an issue at https://github.com/Kuwaaaa/SSLens/issues.
